About

TG is a digital consulting firm with over 15 years of experience in public and global consulting,
connecting AI and data to practical business and operational innovation.
From smart cities and smart farms to IT governance and global consulting,
we deliver execution-focused solutions that connect technology, strategy, and people.

CEO Message

From data to artificial intelligence, and now to quantum computing,
technology continues to evolve without pause.

Yet as the pace of innovation accelerates,
greater confusion and pressure often arise in the field.
While it is easy to say, “We need to adopt AI,”
determining where and how to apply it remains a challenging task.

TG Consulting begins with this very question—
from the perspective of people and business, not technology alone.

We view AI not as an end, but as a means.
By understanding organizations and their work,
we select only those technologies that drive real, meaningful change.

Even in the age of AI, people remain the most valuable asset.
TG is committed to finding harmony between technology and people
and to growing together with our clients as a trusted consulting partner.

Thank you.

Jeon Won Young
Chief Executive Officer, TG Consulting
